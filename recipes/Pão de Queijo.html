Pão de Queijo

Description

Pão de Queijo, or Brazilian cheese bread, is a chewy, cheesy delight often enjoyed at breakfast or as a snack. Made with tapioca flour, these little rolls are naturally gluten-free.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of tapioca flour
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 beaten eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a saucepan, combine milk, water, oil, and salt. Bring to a boil.
  3. Remove from heat and slowly add the tapioca flour, stirring until well combined.
  4. Let the mixture cool slightly, then mix in the grated cheese and beaten eggs until smooth.
  5. Form the dough into small balls and place them on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes, or until the tops are golden brown.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy!
