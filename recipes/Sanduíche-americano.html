Sanduíche americano

Description

A dish very common in Brazil during feasts, birthdays, family meetings. A savory food with soft texture, the taste of chicken arrives in a good combination of elements that will bring delight for your party!

Ingredients

  • 2 packages of sandwich bread
  • 2 cans of heavy cream
  • 2 jars of cream cheese
  • 2 jars of mayonnaise
  • 400 g of chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon of ketchup
  • 1 tablespoon of mustard
  • 1 jar of olives
  • 1 can of mixed vegetables
  • milk to moisten the bread
  • 1 bag of potato sticks (optional)
  • seasoning to taste for the chicken

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken normally, adding seasonings to your taste.
  2. Once the chicken is ready, shred it and add the ketchup, mustard, olives, heavy cream, cream cheese, and mayonnaise. Mix everything.
  3. Blend the mixture in a blender until it forms a dip.
  4. Take a tray (preferably rectangular) and line it with a layer of bread moistened with milk.
  5. Then add a layer of dip and alternate with another layer of bread and another of dip, finishing with a layer of bread (always moistening the bread with a little milk).
  6. Make a cream with the heavy cream, cream cheese, and mayonnaise, and spread it over the top layer of bread and on the sides. Then decorate as you wish.
