Coxinha

Description

A popular Brazilian snack, coxinha is a delicious fried dough filled with seasoned shredded chicken. Perfect for parties and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • 2 cups of shredded chicken breast
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup of cream cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bread crumbs (for coating)
  • 1 egg (beaten)
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken broth and butter to a boil.
  2. Slowly add the flour, stirring constantly until the dough forms and pulls away from the sides of the pot.
  3. Let the dough cool.
  4. In a pan, sauté the onion and garlic until translucent. Add the shredded chicken and cook until heated through.
  5. Mix in the cream cheese, salt, and pepper. Set aside to cool.
  6. Take a small amount of dough, flatten it, and fill with the chicken mixture. Shape into a teardrop form.
  7. Dip the filled dough in beaten egg, then coat with bread crumbs.
  8. Heat the oil in a deep fryer or large pot. Fry the coxinha until golden brown.
  9. Drain on paper towels and serve hot.
