Brigadeiro

Description

A beloved Brazilian sweet treat, often served at birthday parties and festive gatherings. These chocolate fudge balls are rich, sweet, and perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons of unsalted butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles (for rolling)

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine the condensed milk, cocoa powder, and butter over medium heat.
  2. Cook, stirring constantly, until the mixture thickens and starts to pull away from the sides of the pan (about 10 minutes).
  3. Remove from heat and let cool slightly.
  4. Once cool enough to handle, grease your hands with butter and roll the mixture into small balls.
  5. Roll each ball in chocolate sprinkles to coat.
  6. Place in paper cupcake liners and serve.
